8-track is a concept devised by by Hot Since 82 himself which allows artists to create a body of work beyond the limitations of a single or EP.

His own 8-track is a deeply personal, cathartic piece and is the culmination of 2 years work amongst his global touring schedule. His 2 most recent singles 'Buggin'' and Bloodlines feature, plus 6 brand news tracks from right across the house music spectrum.
